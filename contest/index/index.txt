9:T917,<h1>The Contest</h1>
<p>The Benelux Algorithm Programming Contest (BAPC) is a contest in which teams from leading universities in Belgium, the Netherlands, and Luxembourg are served a series of algorithmic problems/puzzles. The goal of each team is to solve the most puzzles as possible within the set time limit. Solutions need to be programmed out on a computer and can be submitted to an automated judging system, after which the solution is checked.</p>
<p>The teams that have solved the most puzzles at the end of the contest qualify for the <a href="https://2024.nwerc.eu/">Northwestern European Regional Contest</a>. In this regional competition the teams can qualify themselves for a ticket to the <a href="https://icpc.global/">International Collegiate Programming Contest</a>, also known as the World Finals.</p>
<p>The BAPC 2024 is hosted by the University of Groningen, and organized by a committee of study association Cover.</p>
