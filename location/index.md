## Location

The GAPC 2024 will be held at the University of Groningen, Netherlands. The event will happen in the Bernoulliborg building on the Zernike campus. The presenations will happen in BB 5161.0151.

```
Bernoulliborg
Nijenborgh 9
9747 AG Groningen
```
